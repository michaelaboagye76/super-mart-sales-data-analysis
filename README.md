# Super-Mart Sales Data Analysis (Excel)

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Super-Mart sales data using **Microsoft Excel**. The goal of the analysis is to uncover insights related to sales performance, profitability, shipping costs, and the effectiveness of marketing channels to support data-driven business decisions.

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query
- Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts

## Dataset Description

The analysis is based on three datasets:

- **orders_data.xlsx** – transactional sales, product, customer, and shipping details
- **returns_data.xlsx** – returned orders information
- **user_data.xlsx** – managerial and regional assignments

### Key Data Fields

- **Order Information:** Order ID, Order Date, Quantity, Priority
- **Financial Metrics:** Sales, Profit, Discount, Unit Price
- **Logistics:** Ship Mode, Shipping Cost, Ship Date
- **Customer Details:** City, State, Region, Segment
- **Product Details:** Category, Sub-category, Supplier
- **Marketing:** Marketing Channel, Product Base Margin
- **Management:** Manager, Region

## Methodology

1. Imported and cleaned all datasets using Excel Power Query.
2. Performed data quality checks to handle errors, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
3. Conducted analysis using Pivot Tables and Charts.
4. Created visualizations to clearly communicate insights and findings.

## Key Metrics Analyzed

- Sales performance across time, regions, products, and customer segments
- Profitability by product, city, and marketing channel
- Shipping and delivery costs by region and delivery method
- Marketing efficiency based on sales and profit contribution

## Key Findings

- Sales targets were achieved in Quarter 1 and Quarter 2 but missed in Quarters 3 and 4 despite growth in sales.
- The South region recorded the highest sales, while the Pacific region had the lowest.
- Technology products contributed the largest share of total sales (51%).
- Truck delivery incurred the highest shipping costs across all regions.
- Email marketing was the most effective channel, generating the highest sales and 61% of total profit.
- Tradeshows and events produced a negative profit contribution.

## Key Insights

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing channels such as Email Marketing and Direct Sales.
- Reduce reliance on costly truck delivery methods where possible.
- Increase targeted marketing in lower-performing regions like Pacific, Central, and Pacific Northwest.
